OPERATION: Data Requirements

The official URL for this operation definition is: 

http://hl7.org/fhir/OperationDefinition/BB-When

When coffee is poured, and milk is offered, it is necessary for the holder of the recipient of milk to say "when" at the time when sufficient milk has been introduced into the coffee. For this purpose, the "WHEN" operation has been added to FHIR.

Parameters

UseNameCardinalityTypeBindingDocumentation
INBevID1..1Reference

The ID of the BrewedBeverage Resource that dictates the beverage to be brewed and the requested additions (if any)

Enough? Say WHEN.
